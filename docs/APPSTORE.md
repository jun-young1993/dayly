**HOME SCREEN WIDGETS**
Place elegant D-Day cards right on your home screen. See your countdown at a glance without opening the app. Supports both iOS WidgetKit and Android App Widgets.

**5 CURATED THEMES**
Choose from carefully designed themes to match your mood:
- Night — deep, calm dark tones
- Paper — warm, classic minimalism
- Fog — soft, muted serenity
- Lavender — gentle, dreamy purple
- Blush — delicate, romantic pink

**MULTIPLE COUNTDOWN STYLES**
Display your countdown your way:
- Days remaining (e.g., "23 days")
- D-minus format (e.g., "D-23")
- Weeks & days (e.g., "3 weeks 2 days")
- Mornings / Nights remaining
- Or hide the number entirely for a minimal look

**SHARE WITH STYLE**
Export your D-Day card as a beautiful image and share it on Instagram, Messages, or any social platform. Your moments deserve to look as good as they feel.

**MILESTONES & NOTES**
Add personal notes and milestone checklists to track your progress toward any event. Stay organized while staying inspired.

**PROGRESS TRACKING**
See how far you've come with a visual progress bar from creation to your target date.

**DESIGNED FOR SIMPLICITY**
No sign-up required. No cloud. No clutter. All your data stays on your device, private and secure.

## Privacy Policy (English)

**Privacy Policy for D-Dayly**

Last updated: March 13, 2026

### Overview

D-Dayly ("the App") is developed by Junyoung Kim. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

### Data Collection and Storage

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

All data you create in D-Dayly — including your D-Day events, notes, milestones, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Device identifiers
- Browsing history
- Contact information

### Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect personal data.

### Local Notifications

D-Dayly may send local notifications to remind you about your D-Day events. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

### Image Sharing

When you use the share feature, D-Dayly generates an image of your D-Day card on your device. The sharing action is handled by your device's native share sheet — D-Dayly does not upload or transmit the image to any server.

### Photos / Gallery Access

If you choose to set a custom background image, the App will request access to your photo library. Selected images are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared with any third party.

### Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all App data by:
- Deleting individual D-Day events within the App
- Uninstalling the App (this removes all associated data)

### Children's Privacy

D-Dayly does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
