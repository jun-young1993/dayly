## Introduction

D-Dayly values your privacy. This app is a widget-first D-Day card and sharing app that operates entirely on your device. No account registration or login is required. We collect only the minimum data necessary to display advertisements.

---

## Information We Collect

D-Dayly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All D-Day calculations, widget configurations, and user inputs are processed and stored locally on your device.

However, the app uses third-party advertising services that may automatically collect certain non-personal information.

Automatically collected data by advertising services may include:

- Device information (model, OS version, language, etc.)
- Advertising ID
- Anonymous usage data related to ad performance

---

## Data Storage

D-Dayly does not operate any servers.

- All widget data (dates, messages, themes) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
- No user data is stored externally.
- No cloud backup is provided.
- All app data remains on your device only.

---

## Device Permissions

D-Dayly may request the following device permissions:

- **Photo Library Access:** To allow users to select images from their gallery for widget customization.
- **Notification Permission:** To send local D-Day reminder notifications. All notifications are scheduled and triggered locally on your device.

These permissions are optional and requested only when the relevant feature is used.

---

## Third-Party Services

D-Dayly uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect non-identifiable information to provide and improve ad performance. For more information, please refer to:

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not control how Google processes advertising data.

---

## Sharing Features

D-Dayly allows users to share D-Day widget cards as images. Shared content is generated locally on your device and transmitted only through the sharing method chosen by the user (e.g., messaging apps, social media). D-Dayly does not store or access any shared content.

---

## Payments and Purchases

D-Dayly does not sell any products or services and does not offer in-app purchases.

---

## Analytics and Tracking

This app does not use analytics services, tracking tools, or crash reporting systems.

---

## Children's Privacy

D-Dayly does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If any such information is discovered, it will be removed immediately.

## Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored on external servers, the risk of data exposure is minimized. All functionality operates locally on your device.
